Favorite Shows

The Fall of the House of Usher
- Aired in 2023: A man dances with the Devil and takes a deal to gain unimaginable wealth. He rises to the top, with a successful company and so much money you can't imagine. He fathers several children, but their fate is doomed. Each episode follows one of his adult children's doom and leads us closer to answering the question: Why is the family dying?
Handmaid's Tale
-Aired in March of 2018-Present: Set in a futuristic post-America timeline, women are stuggling to keep birth numbers up. Women that can bear chidren are ordered to become handmaids to wealthy and "righteous" members of society. This show follows one woman that escapes being a handmaid and breaks down the patriarchy.
Game of Thrones
-Aired from 2011-2019: a fantasy series about a power struggle between families of Westeros and Essos. The story is set against the White Walkers, zombie like figures that will threaten the end of everything if the two families can't unite against them.
Righteous Gemstones
-Aired from 2019- Present: This show casts light on the double life and hypocricay of religous leaders that so often gwt brushed over. It follows a family of a famous pastor, but his children might destroy his legacy. They all partake in dishonorable activities that could destroy their family.
Vice Principals
-Aired from 2016-2018: This show stars Danny McBride and Walton Goggins as two vice principals yearning for the principals position. Throughout the series they go from enemies to friends, back to enemies. Neither are fully cut out for the job, and they will stop at nothing to gain control.
